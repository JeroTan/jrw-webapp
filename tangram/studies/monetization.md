# Digital Monetization Playbook: JRW Simple E-commerce

## 1. Monetization Archetype

- **Model:** Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Retail.
- **Capture Mechanism:** PayMongo (Cards, GCash, Maya) for immediate revenue; Manual COD (Phase 2).

## 2. Value Exchange

- **Creation:** High-quality apparel delivered through a frictionless, "Architectural" digital experience.
- **Capture:** Revenue is captured per unit sold. Transactional integrity (Durable Objects) ensures no revenue is lost to inventory errors.
- **Pre-order Capture:** Allows for revenue capture *before* stock is physically present, improving cash flow for new designs.

## 3. Growth Levers

- **Premium Logistics:** Implementing "Next-Day" delivery tiers as a higher-margin shipping option.
- **Localized Trust:** Expanding into COD (Cash on Delivery) to capture the cash-heavy Philippine market.
- **Inventory Pre-selling:** Using the Pre-order feature to validate new T-shirt designs before committing to full production runs.
